## General

<Accordions>
  <Accordion title="What is the NSTC?">
    National Science Talent Contest (NSTC) is designed to find and reward gifted students in science. The competition consists of a **Screening Test** followed by multiple **Training Camps** to choose the top 4-6 students who represent Pakistan in **International Science Olympiads**.
  </Accordion>

<Accordion title="What is the NSTC Preparations?">
  NSTC Preparations is a first-of-its-kind **online platform** dedicated to
  helping students succeed in the National Science Talent Contest. Our goal is
  to provide students with the **best resources, guidance, and support** to
  reach their full potential and **secure their place as top performers in the
  NSTC**. [Read more](https://nstcprep.blogspot.com/p/about-us_30.html)
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What is the NBTC?">
  The NBTC stands for the **National Biology Talent Contest**, a competition for
  students who have selected **Biology as their primary subject**. It is
  synonymous with NSTC (Biology domain). The Pakistan team of four students
  participating in the **International Biology Olympiad (IBO)** is chosen
  through the NBTC. [Read
  more](https://nstcprep.blogspot.com/2021/06/national-biology-talent-contest-nbtc.html)
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="What is the NPTC?">
  The NPTC specifically refers to the **National Physics Talent Contest** for
  those who choose **Physics as their main subject**. It is simply another name
  for NSTC (Physics domain). The Team Pakistan of five students who participate
  in the **International Physics Olympiad (IPhO)** is selected through NPTC.
  [Read
  more](https://nstcprep.blogspot.com/2022/03/national-physics-talent-contest.html)
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What is the NCTC?">
  The NCTC stands for the **National Chemistry Talent Contest**, a competition
  for students who have selected **Chemistry as their primary subject**. It is
  simply another name for NSTC (Chemistry domain). The Pakistan team of five
  students competing in the **International Chemistry Olympiad (IChO)** is
  selected via the NCTC. [Read
  more](https://nstcprep.blogspot.com/2022/05/national-chemistry-talent-contest.html)
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What is the NMTC?">
  The NMTC refers to the **National Mathematics Talent Contest**, a contest for
  students who have chosen **Mathematics as their main subject**. It is simply
  another name for NSTC (Mathematics domain). The Pakistan team of six students
  participating in the **International Mathematical Olympiad (IMO)** is chosen
  through the NMTC.
</Accordion>

</Accordions>

## Registration

<Accordions>

<Accordion title="Who is eligible to participate in NSTC?">
  
The participant must: 
- Be enrolled in **Pre-9<sup>th</sup>, 9<sup>th</sup>, 10<sup>th</sup>, or 11<sup>th</sup>** grade (Matric, **O Level**, F.Sc Part-I, or **AS Level**). 
- Have a **minimum aggregate of 60%** in core subjects (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, and Biology/Computer) in the last exam. 
- Be **under 20 years** old on 30th June of the Olympiad year.

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What are key dates for registration of NSTC?">
  NSTC registration typically **opens in December** and **closes in February**.
  The deadline to apply for **NSTC-22** is **May 30, 2025**.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can students below Pre-9th grade participate in NSTC?">
  **Exceptionally bright students** can apply if nominated by their institution
  based on factors such as their class performance or exam scores.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How many students can an institution nominate?">
  The institution may nominate any number of students who scored an aggregate of
  60% or more in science subjects. Students can also apply online independently
  from anywhere.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What is the NSTC registration fee?">
  The registration fee for NSTC is **PKR 1000**.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Shall I receive an admit card for the NSTC Screening Test?">
  **Yes**, you will receive an admit card once your fee has been confirmed. You
  can **download** the admit card from the [STEM Careers Programme
  website](https://stem.edu.pk/) close to the test date.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do O/A Level students enter their grades as percentages?">
  O/A Level students should convert their grades in core subjects (Physics,
  Chemistry, Mathematics, Biology/Computer) to a **percentage equivalent**. For
  example: **A\*** = 90%, **A** = 85%, **B** = 75%, **C** = 65%, **D** = 55%,
  **E** = 45%.
</Accordion>

</Accordions>

## Screening Test

<Accordions>

<Accordion title="Is it possible for me to modify the subject in my registration form at a later time?">
  No. Once selected, the subject chosen during the registration process cannot
  be altered or changed.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is it possible to participate in multiple subjects?">
  **No**, you can only participate in **one subject**.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I use a calculator during the Screening Test?">
  **Yes**, you can use a **non-programmable scientific calculator** in the NSTC
  Screening Test.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Where will the NSTC Screening Test be held?">
  The test will be conducted in about **19 major cities** throughout Pakistan,
  including Abbottabad, Bahawalpur, Chitral, D.I. Khan, Faisalabad, Gilgit,
  Gujranwala, Gujrat, Hyderabad, Islamabad/Rawalpindi, Karachi, Lahore, Multan,
  Muzaffarabad, Peshawar, Quetta, Sarghoda, Swat, and Sukkur.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What is the format of the NSTC Screening Test?">
  The test is divided into **three sections**, with the first two sections
  consisting of **multiple-choice questions** and the third section consisting
  of **descriptive questions**.
</Accordion>

</Accordions>

## Training Camps

<Accordions>

<Accordion title="What are NSTC Training Camps?">
  NSTC Training Camps are hosted by the respective **subject's Home
  Institutions** (HI) (Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics). These camps
  are designed to **select and train students** to represent Pakistan in
  **International Science Olympiads**.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Who is eligible to attend Training Camps?">
  **The top 50 students** of each subject, chosen from the results of the
  Screening Test, are invited to participate in the first training camp.
  Subsequent camps have a smaller number of participants chosen through further
  selection.
</Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Where are Training Camps held?">
    The NSTC Training Camps are held at designated Host Institutions (HI) for each subject:
    - **NPTC (Physics):** Pakistan Institute of Engineering and Applied Sciences (**PIEAS**), Islamabad
    - **NCTC (Chemistry):** H.E.J Research Institute of Chemistry, University of Karachi, Karachi
    - **NBTC (Biology):** National Institute for Biotechnology and Genetic Engineering (**NIBGE**), Faisalabad
    - **NMTC (Mathematics):** COMSATS University Islamabad (**CUI**), Lahore Campus
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
