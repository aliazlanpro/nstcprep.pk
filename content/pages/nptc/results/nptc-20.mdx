The **20th National Physics Talent Contest** was a highly anticipated event, held on **March 18, 2023**.
The competition saw the participation of thousands of students from across the country,
each one eager to showcase their skills and knowledge in the field of physics. However,
only a few students were able to make it through the rigorous screening process.
The test was designed to be challenging, and only the **top 45 students**, out of the thousands of participants,
were able to secure a spot for the Training Camps.

The list of students who made it through the Screening Test and qualified for the **1st Training Camp** of the NPTC is available to view. This list serves as a testament to the hard work and dedication of these talented students, and it is a source of inspiration for others who aspire to follow in their footsteps.
